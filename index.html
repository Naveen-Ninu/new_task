our Mission

Jarz is composed of 10,000 unique NFTs (non-fungible tokens) that are made up of 200 exciting visual traits. Holding a Jarz allows a collective member (“holder”) to participate in the management of the Jarz Collective Treasury, which will launch with 20% of our proceeds one day following the public sale

We started Jarz Collective with the end goal in mind of making the cannabis space entirely inclusive.

Roadmap

COMMUNITY WALLET

Complete mint of 10k Jarz, create the Jarz Seed Treasury, and fund it with 20% of proceeds from the mint. 50% of resale royalties will also contribute to the Seed Treasury.

Partnerships and Joint Ventures

The Seed Treasury will be used in collaboration with all Jarz holders to support/fund initiatives decided on together, based on proposals and votes.

Jarz World Studios

In a dedicated effort to promote cannabis education, reform, and advocacy on a major scale, we will produce an animated series featuring many of the Jarz in circulation and more.
